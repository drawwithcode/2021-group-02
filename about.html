The concept of Arcadia is inspired by
the mythological region of the
Peloponnese, which has taken on
the connotations of an idyllic dream
in poetry and mythology.

Arcàdia is a project built in p5.js for the
course Creative Coding at the Politecnico
di Milano byAlexandra Chiojdeanu,
Greta Cozza, Anna Gargantini,
Matteo Ligori, Eva Parenti.
